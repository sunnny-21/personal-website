— About

I make things that feel inevitable.

Products shaped around people. Music pulled from silence. Photographs that hold still long enough to mean something.

There is a thread running through all of it — a belief that the best work does not announce itself. It simply feels right. Earned. Like it could not have been any other way.

I come to design the way a craftsman comes to a bench: with patience, with taste, with the quiet conviction that how something feels is inseparable from what it does.

The sun is my name. Everything radiates outward from a still center.
